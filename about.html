Executive Rooms

An executive suite in its most general definition is a collection of offices or rooms—or suite—used by top managers of a business—or executives. Over the years, this general term has taken on a variety of specific meanings.

Deluxe Rooms

The sink is in-room with toilet & shower separately enclosed. Deluxe Queen, Triple, King, and Quad rooms are more comparable in size to a modern day hotel room, offering a city view & full sized bathroom, many with a bathtub & shower. ... The de luxe rooms are large with separate sitting area.

luxury Rooms

A luxury room in a hotel or other public accommodation such as a cruise ship denotes, according to most dictionary definitions, connected rooms under one room number. ...
